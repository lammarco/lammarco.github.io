About Me

Background

Hi, I'm Marco Lam, someone who is passionate about design and technical achievements in games. After graduating from UC Irvine with degrees in both Computer Science (Specialization: Intelligent Systems) and Computer Game Science, I am currently job-searching while working on DeltaPhysics.

Passion for Games

My early passion came from a fortunate childhood exposed to a large variety of games. From Pokemon and Megaman series on the DS, to various flavors of MMOs, not to mention an emerging field of mobile games, my curiosity was easily occupied by these entertainment.

I love technical aspects of games, which not only includes glitch-hunting and technical tricks, but also automation in technical games and stat optimizations. An example would be Luanti (formerly Minetest), not just for its room for creativity, but also for its capacity as a mod engine. Of course, mesecon and pipeworks are a must (think: redstone builds in Minecraft).

Realizing Ideas with Programming

With the drive to understand the workings of games and to formulate my own ideas, I included programming into my studies. In addition to providing rules and principles the videogames rely on, the field also taught me problem-solving and helped me translate my game ideas into coded logic. More recently, my interests in automation and optimization also led to the specialization in machine learning, which assisted in adapting to the recent LLM boom.
